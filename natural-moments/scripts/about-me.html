About Me

Photography has always been a part of my life. My father loves to take photgraphs so ever since I was little I would be borrowing his camera. The first time I really got into photography was when I was 8 years old at my cousins wedding in Texas. My dad brought his expensive camera and let me sneak around with it and take photos of the beautiful event.

My favorite medium to photograph is nature. Growing up on 20 acres of woods really taught me how to appreciate the outdoors. I want people to see the same beauty that I do when I'm out in nature.I photograph for fun at the moment but someday hope to be able to see prints and/or create paintings of my shots.
