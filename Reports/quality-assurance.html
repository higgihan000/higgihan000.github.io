    What does quality assurance mean for web site development?

    It ensures that the end users have a functional user interface and best user experience when uding a website. They uncover flaws that could have been overlooked during design and development.

    Describe the two key principles of quality assurance: "Fit for Purpose" and "Right First Time."

    "Fit for Purpose" mean that product should be suitable for the intended purpose. "Right First Time" means mistakes should be eliminated.

    How do web standard relate to web quality?

    They relate to web quality because they are rules and guidelines established by the W3C to promote consistency in the design code which makes up a web page.

    Describe what web accessibility means. What steps can you take to make your page more accessible?

    Accessibility means people should be able to perceive, undertsand, navigate, and interact with the web. Some ways to make your website accessible is to make sure your site is keyboard friendly, make sure all content is easily accessible, add alt text to all images, chose colors carefully, and use headers to structure your content correctly.
